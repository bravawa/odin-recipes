Pad Gra Prao Recipe

I make this dish all the time! You should try it if you love the sweetness & kick of thai peppers, the saltiness of soy sauce, and the umagi of some delicious extra-lean ground beef... topped with a beautifully fried golden egg.

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp of your favorite oil
  • 1/3 cup of jasmine rice
  • 200g of extra-lean ground beef
  • some garlic, onion, green & red thai peppers, basil
  • 1 tbsp of oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp of light soy sauce
  • 1 tsp of dark soy sauce
  • 1 tsp of fish sauce
  • 1 extra-large egg

Steps

  1. fry some of your favorite oil on high heat
  2. on the side, prepare your rice
  3. toss your onions, peppers, garlic until garlic browns
  4. throw that delicious ground beef in the wok and let it sit for a few minutes until the ground beef browns
  5. start mixing the ground beef with the beautiful ingredients you had and keep mixing until the ground beef looks half-cooked
  6. sauce it up! put in all the sauces i mentioned above
  7. wait until your ground beef looks ready, then put it on your bed of rice
  8. fry that egg
